These Chinese dumplings have a delicious filling with a sauce that complements the whole dish.

In order to make such a dish, you will need patience and love!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 2 chopped green onion
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons rice wine
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • peanut oil
  • 1 cup chicken broth divided
  • 25-30 wonton skins

    • Steps

    1. Mix first 10 ingredients in a bowl.
    2. Take a wonton wrapper and place 1 heaping teaspoon of filling in center.
    3. Moisten edges with water, fold over and press to seal edges.
    4. Add 3 tablespoons of Peanut Oil to a large skillet and saute until the bottoms are golden brown.
    5. When golden brown, add 1/2 cup chicken broth, cover pan and simmer 8 minutes.
    6. Remove from pan.
    7. Reserve broth in a separate bowl and repeat process until all potstickers are cooked.
    8. Keep warm on a plate covered.
