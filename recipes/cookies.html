Chocolate Chip Cookies

These cookies are so good that you must try if you have a sweet tooth. You can add any kind of toppings and create with many flavors. For the sake of being basic, we are gonna bake chocolate chip cookies.

In order to make such a dish, you will need patience and love!

Ingredients

  • Melted butter
  • Brown and white sugars
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg yolk
  • Egg
  • Baking soda
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate chips

    • Steps

    1. Beat the butter and sugars together to get a nice fluffy texture.
    2. Add egg and vanilla and mix for a little bit.
    3. Mix in the dry ingredients.
    4. Add chocolate chips and fold them into the mixture.
    5. Roll the dough into balls and bake them in the oven.
