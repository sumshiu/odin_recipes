Cake

The picture may be lying about it being a chocolate cake but we are going to make a vanilla cake.

In order to make such a dish, you will need patience and love!

Ingredients

  • Plain or all purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk, full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

    • Steps

    1. Beat the eggs with sugar for a whole 7 minutes until thick and glossy, it changes from yellow to white, and tripled in volume (just over double depth in the bowl).
    2. Whisk together the flour, baking powder and salt. Then add into egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on Speed 1 in between. This should make the flour just incorporated – don’t worry if there are some bits not mixed in.
    3. use a microwave or stove to heat milk with the butter until it melts.
    4. Mix some batter into hot milk.
    5. Slowly pour milk mixture back inot whipped eggs over 20 seconds on Speed 1. Scrape down sides of bowls, then mix for 10 seconds on Speed 1 – the batter should now be smooth;.
    6. Pour into 2 cake pans lined with parchment paper.
    7. Knock out big air bubbles.
    8. Bake 30 minutes til golden brown.
    9. Cool upside down.
